Welcome to the About Us Page

Torakusu Yamaha repaired an organ at an elementary school in Hamamatsu, and he succeeded in producing an organ in the same year. The Yamaha brand trademark "Yamaha" is derived from the name of the company's founder, Torakusu Yamaha, who was a pioneer in the manufacture of Western-style musical instruments.

Here is a short timeline of our journey.

  • 1887- Birth of the Yamaha Brand
  • 1889 - Establishes Yamaha Fukin Seizoujo (currently Yamaha Corporation)
  • 1897 - Establishes Nippon Gakki Co., Ltd. (currently Yamaha Corporation)
