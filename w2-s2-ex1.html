My Lovely Trip to Siem Reap!

18 October 2024

During my stay in Siem Reap for the past two months, I have done many interesting activities. First, I went to the famous temple, Angkor Wat, and took many pictures. Then, I drove through the Angkor Thom area while riding a tuk tuk. I also visited Neak Porn temple, and it was really beautiful during the rainy season.

I then stayed home for a few more weeks until it was Pchum Ben holiday. During Pchum Ben, I went to the pagoda with my friends, and I visited Angkor Wat again that day. Then, the next day, I went to the pagoda with my mother.

Conclusion

My stay in Siem Reap was very fruitful because I got to spend more time with my friends and family. I also travelled a lot more than I have before.
